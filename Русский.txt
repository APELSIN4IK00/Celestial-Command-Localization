#523# Now about movement

You may rotate your ship by keys A and D. Try to rotate your ship a bit left and right.
#524# Note that your ship located in vacuum, there is no air friction, so it will keep its linier and angular velocity unil you will stop it manually.
#525# Automatic stabilization is very useful if you want to stop rotation of your ship. Activate stabilizator by pressing highlitted button or its hotkey
#526# While stabilizer is active, it will automatically use an engines to stop rotation of the ship.

However, make note, that your fuel is limited. These basic maneuvering engines consumes liquid hydrogen. Your current amount of luquid hydrogen displayed on the screen (highligther by arrow)
#527# While stabilzier is active, it will stop rotation everytime, even if you don't really need it, this will increase the consumption of fuel. so it could be useful to disable stabilizrer if you don't need it.

Disable stbilizer by pressing R hotkey.

#531# Now display relative velocity
#532# While this tool is active and you have selected an objects, you will see two arrows near your ship.

White arrow - direction to selected target
Orrange arrow - velocity vector of your ship relative to selected target
#533# Now, in order to approach to selected target, begin to move your ship toward a target.

You need to align orrange arrow with the same direction as white arrow.
#534# Good. Once two arrows aligned, it means that your ship moves right toward the target.

Keep an eye on the distance to target. Do not crush into it.
Once you will approach, decelerate your ship until yellow arrow wil became very short. It will means that your relative velocity is almost zero.

Approach to selected target and stop your ship near it.
